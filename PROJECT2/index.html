Branding ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

This is a company selling clothes, accessories, and home decor. This website will offer detailed product information, including cloth textures, costs, brand, size and colour, and will provide customers with a clear image of the product. It also offers a shopping feature where customers can add the product to a shopping cart and then check out. Ideally, this website will also have parcel tracking and customer service.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Potential customers for this company include women from 18-38 who shop for clothes regularly. One specific example would be a woman named Maggie, who is 24 years old and likes to do online shopping. Moreover, the company, to her as a person, would be someone who has a keen eye for design and daily outfits, which means the company is fashionable, hip and has a vivid and colourful vibe. The company is also young and energetic, with diverse options for the customers to choose from, making it stand out from its competitors.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

The company attempts to create an excellent visual website with bright colours that attract users' attention and uses a clear navigation bar to help users quickly find the product they want. Ideally, the website will also provide similar products for the customers. Moreover, the company will have a distinguishable and memorable value which fits the young customers' core value and attracting marketing strategy on its website. It also has professional customer service, which will meet customers' expectations, gaining numerous positive feedback from the users.
